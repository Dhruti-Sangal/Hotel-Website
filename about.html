About Our Hotel

"Description about your hotel"

It is a warm and intimate place, open all year round, where every detail reflects refinement and attention to beauty. Very close to the sea and right in the centre of Riccione, it is a model of a refined and embracing hospitality, the answer for those who love to experience a relaxing holiday made up of lunches in the greenery of the veranda, enjoy Junior Suites perfect for a large family or a splendid attic overlooking the sea.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Luxury and elegance, refined and relaxing environment where everything is meant to be lived to the fullest!

Excellent cuisine, typical dishes from the best Romagna tradition and more!

An exclusive beach at your service “Beach 88/89” with Swimming pools, mini-club and many other services

Many dedicated services for our youngest guests. Fun for kids and relaxation is assured for the parents!

With us is a continual party! Theme party in our beatiful locations; to surprise you and make you live exciting moments to remembers us forever

Romagna hospitality, typical and much appreciated! Qualified staff whose sole objective is total customer satisfaction!
